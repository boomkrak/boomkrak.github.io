Week of Jan 30

New Tools
  • Mockup Generatorisp
    Mockup Generator is a macOS app that sits in your menu bar allowing you to capture screenshots of your favourite websites and wrap them in device mock-ups.
  • Looped Live Mockups
    You can make live mockup with smooth animation with Looped.
  • Fullstory
    With Fullstory you can capture every click, swipe, and scroll. Session replay tells you everything you want to know about your customer’s online experience.
  • ProdPad
    With ProdPad you can build and share your flexible product roadmap, and prioritize your backlog.
  • Bizplan
    Bizplan is modern business planning software for startups.
  • Mockflow
    Actually the product is not only a wireframing tools but also included another tools like design collaboration, styleguide creator, banner maker, sitemap maker, and website builder.
  • Element
    Element, a Vue 2.0 based component library for developers, designers and product managers, with a set of design resources.
